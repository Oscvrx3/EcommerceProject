The Brief History of Cleats

Today, the black soccer cleat or the football boot, is a staple when playing the game. However, the soccer cleats that we know todays hasnt always been like that. The soccer cleat has evolved from a pair of boots that King Henry the VIII of England wore. From there, the pair of boots evolved to the cleats that even average people can now wear.
